About Me

After teaching myself how to code, I received my first opportunity to work as a Junior Developer at Waracle, but before life as a developer, I worked as a mechanical engineer in the construction industry for 4 years. It is in this role where I developed a very technical mindset as I was responsible for carrying out complex calculations, designing large mechanical systems for buildings and overseeing the installation of these designs on multi-million pound projects. However, my passion in life lies in technology, and it is this reason why I decided to make a career switch.


My time at Waracle has taught me a lot about what is required of a professional software developer. I have had the pleasure of working with some amazing developers and have used this opportunity to learn as much as I can and really hone my skills. This has allowed me to become a proficient Python developer as I have been involved in developing a data engineering tool, which transforms millions of lines of raw data into a readable format for analysis by a team of Data Scientists. Working on this project has reinforced to me the importance of having good unit and integration tests, and has allowed me to write my own tests using pytest. I have also gained a working knowledge of the DevOps side, learning how to create new docker containers and how to deploy our tool into a kubernetes cluster on Azure. I have created microservices, and interacted with various APIs to send and receive data from various different places. All in all, I've loved every minute working here, and I am currently looking for a new and exciting opportunity for me to further improve my skills.
